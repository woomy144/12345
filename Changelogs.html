Woomy-Arras.io Official In-Game Rules:
  1. No scripting, exploit abuse, bug abuse, or abusing other unfair advantages.
  2. No using inappropriate, insulting, or offensive names.
  3. No abusing beta-tester/token permissions for advantages or unwanted effects.
  4. No use of Arras Controller commands for harm, such as harassment.
  5. No intentionally lagging/crashing servers.
  6. Listen to staff if they tell you to stop doing something in-game.
  7. No cheating to gain score for world records in any way.
  8. No purposely witch-hunting players to deny them of their scores.
  • Be warned that breaking any of these rules may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the game.
